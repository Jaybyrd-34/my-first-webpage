Some favorite places

There are so many beautiful places in the world, heck even in your own backyard there are at least 12(lol). I have traveled some in my 34 years, mostly in my own country and I have checked of 25 states and 3 countries to be exact. I can tell you that all of these places had beauty, some profound and some it took effort to find, but they were all beautiful in their own way. Of all the places, I would have to say that my home of 6 years tops the list. Oregon is special. It has a little bit of everything. You want pristine ocean beach, You got it! How about mountain lakes hidden deep in a lush green forest, You got it! Wanna get lost in the desert and trip balls on mushrooms, No problem!

Here is a list of just a few of my favorite places that lie right outside my door.

  1. My backyard, because its my own and filled with family
  2. The willamette river, its easy to access and always refreshing
  3. Any of the many secluded beaches on the oregon coast
